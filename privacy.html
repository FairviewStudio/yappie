Our Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

Your privacy is important to us. To better protect your privacy we provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used.

The Information We Collect

Any information gathered by us is used to customize the experience for you the user, and is not shared or sold to any third parties, except as expressly provided for in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

100% Local Storage

All information collected by Yappie is stored exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • All conversations and chat history
  • Teaching data and personality customization
  • User preferences and settings
  • Any personal information you provide

We do not upload, transmit, or store any of your data on our servers. Your privacy is protected because your data never leaves your device.

Our Commitment to Data Security

Yappie uses local-only storage architecture. All your data, including conversations, teaching inputs, and personal information, is stored directly on your device using secure local storage mechanisms. This means your information never travels over the internet to our servers, providing an additional layer of security and privacy protection.

To prevent unauthorized access and maintain data accuracy, we use industry standard methods to safeguard and secure the information stored locally on your device. We believe in protecting your information just as we would want our information protected.

In the event that personal information is compromised as a result of a breach of security, we will promptly notify those persons whose personal information has been compromised as may be required by applicable law.

Please note that information you enter for your user profile or in your posts may be accessed and viewed publicly and therefore you should not post information that you consider confidential or that you do not want distributed publicly. Although in some areas you will have the ability to edit or delete information, please note that even this information may have been cached on servers, cut and pasted or otherwise incorporated into other posts or for other uses, and so once information is posted you should consider that it may be publicly available perpetually, and in different locations and/or media than your original submission.

Notifications

It is our policy to provide notifications, whether such notifications are required by law or are for marketing or other business related purposes, to our users via email notice, written or hard copy notice, or through conspicuous posting of such notice via our website or related apps, as determined by us in our sole discretion. Not withstanding the foregoing, we reserve the right to determine the form and means of providing notifications to our users.

Child Protection

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, we will purge any information we receive from people we believe to be children under 13 from our database and cancel the corresponding accounts.

Use of User Information

Important: Because all data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to your conversations, teaching data, or personal information. Information you provide within the app is used solely by the AI learning engine to improve your personalized experience with Yappie.

We do not collect, transmit, or store IP addresses, cookies, or any tracking information. We do not share, sell, or provide any user data to third parties because we simply do not have access to it—it all remains on your device.

Future Account Login Features

Currently, Yappie operates without requiring user accounts or login credentials. All data is stored locally on your device, and no account registration is necessary to use the app.

However, we may introduce optional account login features in the future to provide enhanced functionality such as:

  • Cross-device synchronization of your Yappie's personality and data
  • Cloud backup and restore capabilities
  • Access to premium features or content
  • Enhanced support and customer service

Important: If we introduce account login features in the future, they will be completely optional. You will be able to continue using Yappie without creating an account, and all your data will continue to be stored locally on your device.

Should you choose to create an account in the future, we will:

  • Only collect the minimum information necessary for account creation (such as email address)
  • Use industry-standard encryption to protect your account credentials
  • Provide clear options for what data, if any, you wish to sync across devices
  • Allow you to delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Update this Privacy Policy to reflect any changes in data collection practices

We will notify users in advance of any account login feature implementation and provide detailed information about what data would be collected, how it would be used, and how it would be protected. Your privacy and data security will remain our top priority, regardless of whether you choose to use account features or continue with local-only storage.

Compliance

Access to your Personal Information

You have a right to access, modify, correct and request that we internally delete any Personal Information provided to or obtained by the app. If you update, modify or correct any Personal Information, we may keep copies of your Personal Information prior to such update, modification or correction for uses provided for in this Policy. You may update, modify or correct your Personal Information via your account Preferences. Alternatively, you may contact support@fvworks.com for updates, modifications and corrections to your Personal Information, or to request access to your Personal Information.

Retention Of Your Personal Information

Since all your Personal Information is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the app's built-in data deletion features. When you delete data from the app, it is permanently removed from your device.

We do not store your Personal Information on our servers, so there is no need to request deletion from us. Your data is yours alone, stored only on your device, and you can manage it directly through the app.

Protection of Your Personal Information

Any Personal Information that you provide to us will be protected in a commercially reasonable manner.

Notice of Breach of Security

Because all your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers, the risk of a data breach through our systems is eliminated. Your information is protected by your device's built-in security features. However, we recommend that you keep your device secure with up-to-date software and appropriate security measures.

In the unlikely event that we discover any security vulnerability in the app itself that could affect local data storage, we will notify users as soon as possible and provide updates to address the issue.

How to Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us email at fvworks@hotmail.com.
